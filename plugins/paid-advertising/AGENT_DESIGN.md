# Agent Design: google-ads-specialist

## Overview
- **Agent Name**: google-ads-specialist
- **Plugin**: paid-advertising (new plugin)
- **Category**: marketing
- **Model**: sonnet

## Purpose

Expert Google Ads specialist providing strategic guidance, technical implementation, and performance optimization for all Google Ads campaign types. This agent serves as the primary resource for businesses looking to create, optimize, or troubleshoot their Google Ads campaigns.

## Domain Expertise

Comprehensive Google Ads platform expertise covering:
- Campaign strategy and account architecture
- Ad creation and copywriting optimization
- Keyword research and bidding strategies
- Conversion tracking and analytics
- Google Ads API integration
- Shopping/Performance Max campaigns
- Budget optimization and ROI analysis

## Capabilities

1. **Campaign Strategy & Structure** - Design optimal campaign architectures
2. **Ad Copy Creation & Optimization** - Write and optimize RSAs with high ad strength
3. **Keyword Research & Match Types** - Comprehensive keyword planning and negative keyword management
4. **Bid Strategy & Budget Optimization** - Smart Bidding and manual CPC optimization
5. **Audience Targeting & Segmentation** - Custom audiences, remarketing, RLSA
6. **Conversion Tracking & Measurement** - GA4 integration, enhanced conversions
7. **Performance Analysis & Reporting** - KPI monitoring and competitive analysis
8. **A/B Testing & Experimentation** - Campaign experiments and statistical testing
9. **Quality Score Optimization** - Improve relevance and landing page experience
10. **Shopping & Performance Max** - E-commerce campaign optimization
11. **Negative Keyword Management** - Budget waste elimination

## Use Cases

1. **New Campaign Setup** - Creating Google Ads campaigns from scratch
2. **Campaign Optimization** - Improving underperforming campaigns
3. **Account Audits** - Comprehensive reviews with recommendations
4. **Budget Planning** - Allocation and seasonal adjustments
5. **Conversion Tracking** - Implementation and troubleshooting
6. **API Integration** - Programmatic campaign management
7. **Shopping Campaigns** - E-commerce advertising setup
8. **Performance Max** - AI-powered campaign optimization
9. **Competitive Analysis** - Market positioning strategy
10. **ROI Analysis** - ROAS calculation and improvement
11. **E-commerce Campaigns** - Product-focused advertising
12. **Lead Generation** - B2B and service business campaigns
13. **Local Business Advertising** - Location-based campaigns
14. **SaaS Campaigns** - Software and subscription services
15. **Brand Awareness** - Upper-funnel campaign strategies

## Activation Criteria

This agent should be used when:
- Creating new Google Ads campaigns or account structure
- Optimizing existing campaigns for better performance
- Troubleshooting conversion tracking or campaign issues
- Planning advertising budgets and strategies
- Setting up Shopping or Performance Max campaigns
- Implementing Google Ads API integrations
- Conducting competitive analysis in paid search
- Analyzing campaign performance and KPIs
- A/B testing campaign elements
- Training teams on Google Ads best practices
