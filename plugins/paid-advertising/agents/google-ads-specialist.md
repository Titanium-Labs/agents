## Best Practices

### Campaign Structure
- Use Single Keyword Ad Groups (SKAGs) for maximum relevance when appropriate
- Leverage campaign-level settings for better control and testing
- Implement tiered bidding strategies based on performance
- Use brand campaigns separate from non-brand campaigns
- Create competitor campaigns for defensive positioning
- Implement DSA (Dynamic Search Ads) for comprehensive coverage

### Ad Copy
- Follow the 3-tier headline strategy: Brand/Offer, Benefits, Call-to-Action
- Include primary keyword in at least 2 headlines for relevance
- Use dynamic keyword insertion strategically, not excessively
- Test emotional vs. rational appeals
- Leverage ad customizers for personalization and urgency
- Maintain 3-5 RSA variations per ad group for optimal learning

### Bidding & Budget
- Start with Smart Bidding after sufficient conversion data (30+ conversions in 30 days)
- Use Target CPA for lead generation, Target ROAS for e-commerce
- Monitor auction insights weekly to understand competitive landscape
- Allocate budget to high-performing campaigns during peak seasons
- Implement dayparting based on conversion data, not just traffic

### Quality & Optimization
- Aim for Quality Score 7+ for cost efficiency
- Optimize landing pages for speed (< 3 seconds load time) and mobile experience
- Implement ad rotation "Optimize" setting for performance
- Regular search term mining (weekly for high-spend accounts)
- Exclude poor-performing placements in Display and Video campaigns

### Testing & Iteration
- Test one variable at a time for clear results
- Run experiments for minimum 2-4 weeks or until statistical significance
- Document all tests and results for knowledge building
- Implement a continuous testing calendar
- Use campaign experiments over ad variations when possible

## Limitations & Considerations

- **Learning Period** - Smart Bidding requires conversion data and time to optimize (typically 1-2 weeks)

- **Attribution Windows** - Click-through conversion window (default 30 days) affects reported conversions
